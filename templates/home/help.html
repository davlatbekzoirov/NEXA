Do I need one account for every module?
Yes — a single sign-up gives you access to your grades, budget, involvement log, and application tracker under one profile.
How does the study scheduler pick my time blocks?
It distributes an assignment's estimated prep time across the days leading up to its due date, respecting the daily hours and time window you set on the Schedule page. Changing an assignment's due date or hours regenerates its blocks automatically.
How is the spending runway calculated?
It measures your daily non-essential spending velocity over a trailing 14-day window and projects forward against your fixed costs and the semester end date.
Can I share a document with a counselor who doesn't have an account?
Yes — generate a secure, view-only reviewer link with a 1–30 day expiry from the Documents page. You can revoke it at any time.
Where do deadline reminder emails come from?
A scheduled job checks daily for deadlines 30, 14, and 7 days out and emails you automatically, skipping applications already marked Accepted or Rejected.
