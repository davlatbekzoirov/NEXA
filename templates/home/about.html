Why NEXA exists

Most students juggle their academics in one app, their budget in a notes file, their club hours in memory, and their college or grad-school applications in a spreadsheet that's always slightly out of date. NEXA started as four separate tools built to solve each of those problems properly — and grew into one connected system, because the problems were never really separate.

How we build

Every module is designed around the same idea: track the real thing, not a simplified version of it. That means a debt simplifier that actually minimizes transactions, a grade calculator that runs What-If and Target Grade scenarios, and a deadline engine that emails you before it's too late — not a generic to-do list wearing a school-themed skin.
