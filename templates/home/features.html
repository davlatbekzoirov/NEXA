SmartStudy

Weighted grade tracking per course, a What-If calculator, a Target Grade solver, an automatic study block scheduler, a Pomodoro timer tied to your courses, and burnout alerts when you've been overdoing it.

CampusCribs

A predictive spending-runway calculator that warns you before rent money runs out, a greedy debt-simplification algorithm for splitting bills with roommates, and a chore tracker with photo proof.

CampusPulse

Track club roles from member to president, log volunteer hours toward Bronze through Platinum tiers, and auto-generate resume bullets in the Google X-Y-Z format from your own impact journal.

UniTracker

Manage every application with Reach/Match/Safety labeling, dynamic admissions-odds estimates, document versioning with secure reviewer share links, test score validation, and automated deadline emails.
